About Our Institution

Quadrant Institute of technology is an advanced tech school founded by Quadrant Softwares LTD. The Institute’s facility is located in Eldoret, Kenya. QIT has served as the company’s coding school where we refined our developer’s skill in varied modern software development technologies prior to absorption at the company. Gradually, QIT has grown into a tech center of repute in the Kenyan IT circles.

At Quadrant Institute of Technology, we believe in everyone’s potential. Schooling has evolved from blackboard & chalk to modern learning. QIT is where we teach you the theory to help you understand the phenomena behind current technologies and immediately immerse our students on a practical, hands-on training. Where they get to experience the Market relevance of what they learn in our current digital era.
